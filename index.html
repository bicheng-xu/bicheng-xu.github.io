Biography

Bicheng Xu is a Master of Science in Computer Science student at the University of British Columbia under the supervision of Prof. Leonid Sigal. Previously, he received a Bachlor of Engineering degree in Computer Science and Technology from Zhejiang University (2017), and a Bachlor of Science degree (with distinction) in Applied Sciences from Simon Fraser University (2017) with major in Computer Science and minor in Mathematics. His research interests are in Computer Vision, its intersection with Natural Language Processing, and Machine Learning.
